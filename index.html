What about email deliverability?

  • Using secondary domains is essential for sending outreach emails. If you're currently using your primary domain, it's advisable to stop, as this can significantly harm your domain's overall reputation
  • Instead, we set up secondary domains that redirect to your main domain and create mailboxes to handle the email sending
  • Setting up this email infrastructure requires time for warming up, but ensures that your primary domain's reputation remains protected, regardless of what happens with these secondary domains
